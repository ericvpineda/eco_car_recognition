Did You Know?

  • According to the International Energy Agency (IEA), the transportation sector accounts for approximately 24% of global energy-related carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions, with cars being a significant contributor.
  • A single gallon of gasoline burned by an average car emits around 20 pounds (9 kilograms) of CO2 into the atmosphere, contributing to climate change and air pollution.
  • The harmful emissions from conventional cars, including nitrogen oxides (NOx) and particulate matter (PM), have adverse effects on human health, leading to respiratory issues and other illnesses.
